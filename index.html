Thai Massage Therapy

Enjoy various health benefits of a Traditional Thai Massage which focuses on stretching, pulling and rocking movements. It incorporates use of the therapist’s hands, knees, legs and feet to apply deep muscle compression, joint mobilization and acupressure to promote relaxation, pain relief, flexibility, improved circulation, and a boosted immune system..

Thai Facial Treatment

Thai facial therapy is a type of massage that combines elements of traditional Thai massage with facial massage techniques. The goal of Thai facial therapy is to promote relaxation, improve circulation, Detoxification and enhance the overall health and appearance of the skin.

Herbal compress

Herbal compress, is a technique that involves applying hot or cold compresses to the body, infused with a blend of herbs and essential oils. This technique can help to relieve muscle tension and pain, reduce inflammation, improve circulation, and promote relaxation.

Hot Stone Therapy

Hot stone therapy is a relaxing and effective massage technique that uses heated stones to soothe sore muscles, promote relaxation, and improve circulation. By placing smooth, flat stones on specific points of the body, and using them to massage muscles, hot stone therapy can help to ease tension and pain, reduce stress and anxiety, and improve overall well-being.
